Item List

  • Guide on how to use this app

  • Input an item and press the "Enter" key or click the "Add Item" button to add it to the list.
  • To remove an item, click the "X" button next to it.
  • To filter the list, type a search term in the filter Items field.
  • To clear all items, click the "Clear All Items" button.
  • To change brightness, click the "moon" icon.
  • To edit an item in the list (eg to correct a typo), click on the item.
  • Click on the "?" icon to hide/see this guide.
